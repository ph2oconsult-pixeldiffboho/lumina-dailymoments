# Real-World System Refinement: The Path (v1.0)

## 1. Updated System Rules (The "Messy Day" Layer)

*   **The "Good Enough" Principle:** A day is successful if the app is opened. Completion is optional; presence is the goal.
*   **Non-Linear Continuity:** If a user returns after 3+ days, the app acknowledges the gap with warmth ("Glad you're back, we missed you") and continues the thread without guilt or "catch-up" work.
*   **Low-Stakes Interaction:** Every interactive step must have a "Just Listen" or "Skip" option. No "forced" participation.
*   **Energy-Aware UI:** If it's late (e.g., after 8 PM), the app should offer a "Low Energy Mode" toggle on the first screen.

---

## 2. Fallback Interaction Examples (Handling Resistance)

| Scenario | Fallback Response |
| :--- | :--- |
| **"I don't know"** | "That's a big 'I don't know'. Sometimes feelings are just fuzzy shapes without names. Let's just sit with the fuzziness for a second." |
| **"I don't want to"** | "Totally fair. Some days are 'not today' days. We'll just leave this here for when you're ready. Want to just look at the [Imagery] for a moment?" |
| **Silence (No Input)** | "Silence is a good place to be. We're just here together. No words needed. Tap whenever you're ready to move on." |
| **Distraction** | "It's okay if your mind is elsewhere. Let's just take one breath together and call it a night." |

---

## 3. Low-Energy Flow Example (The "Quick Moment")

**Day 2: Reality (Low Energy Version)**
*   **Screen 1:** "Tonight is a 'quiet' night. Let's just notice the [Metaphor, e.g., The Storm] for one breath." (Slow, calming animation of the imagery).
*   **Screen 2:** "It's there. You're here. That's enough for tonight. You did the hard part just by showing up. See you tomorrow."

---

## 4. Parent Reality Guidance (The "Whisper" Mode)

*   **The Presence Prompt:** "You don't need to say anything right now. Just your shoulder against theirs is the medicine."
*   **The Permission Prompt:** "It's okay if they aren't 'getting it' yet. You're planting a seed, not building a house."
*   **The Self-Compassion Prompt:** "If you're too tired to lead this, just let the app do the talking. You're allowed to just be the audience tonight."
*   **The "Fail" Reframe:** "If they walk away mid-session, that's okay. You showed them that this space exists. That's the win."

---

## 5. Updated Quality Rules

*   **Disengagement-Proof:** The journey must still provide value even if the child never says a word.
*   **Guilt-Free Skipping:** Skipping a day must feel like a valid choice, not a failure of the "system."
*   **Minimalist Interaction:** No more than one "required" tap per day. Everything else should be optional.
*   **Emotional Safety:** The app must never sound "disappointed" in the user.
