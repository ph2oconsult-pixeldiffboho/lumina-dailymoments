# User Testing Plan: The Path (v1.0)

## 1. Test Setup
*   **Participants:** 6–8 families (mix of ages, focusing on the "Guided" layer first).
*   **Duration:** 7 days (one full journey cycle).
*   **Primary Journey:** "Feeling Stuck" (The Heavy Day).
    *   *Why:* It’s the most universal entry point for families seeking emotional support.

## 2. What to Measure (The "Signals")
*   **The Hook:** Day 1 → Day 2 return rate. (Do they come back after the first experience?)
*   **The Friction:** Time spent in Onboarding vs. Time to start the first activity.
*   **The Drop-off:** Which specific Day (1–7) or Step (within a day) do users stop?
*   **The Habit:** Percentage of users who use the "Daily Practice" vs. the main Journey.

## 3. Questions to Ask (The "Human Side")
*   **Effort:** "When did opening the app feel like 'one more thing' you had to do?"
*   **Ease:** "When did the app feel like it just 'slid' into your evening routine?"
*   **Resistance:** "Was there a specific night you *didn't* want to use it? What stopped you?"
*   **Connection:** "Did the app actually spark a conversation, or did it feel like you were just clicking through?"

## 4. Success Criteria
*   **Retention:** At least 60% of families reach Day 4.
*   **Onboarding:** Users complete the setup and start Day 1 in under 90 seconds.
*   **Frictionless Entry:** "Easy to start" is the most common feedback for the Home screen.
*   **Completion:** At least 2 families complete the full 7-day journey.

---
**Goal:** Validate if "The Path" is a tool families *want* to use, or just a tool they *think* they should use.
